Products and Services

Other Training

Cosmos eLearning has created a curriculum that will help any organization provide the training necessary to keep your employees safe and ensure compliance within the regulatory Environmental Health, Safety and Security environment. On occasion additional training is needed to meet the hands on skills needs of your team. In those cases Cosmos Consulting is prepared to deliver the necessary training through a variety of different mediums.
Learning Management System

Providing initial and refresher training is a critical beginning to ensuring your team works safely and is compliant with all regulatory requirements.  Going beyond the actual training by providing you documentation and review capabilities is critical in helping you manage the training function. Our Learning Management System (LMS) enables efficient and productive oversight of your training needs. It even allows you to add in house training documentation to each employees training records.
Consulting Services

LCosmos Consulting will assist you in determining what training is best suited for your work environment. If hands on or instructor led training is better suited to meet the risks your organization is exposed to you can be assured Cosmos will work with you to insure the proper training curriculum is designed. Cosmos Consulting also provides premium consulting services for every element of your Environmental Health, Safety and Security Program.
Other Products

Cosmos Consulting provides additional creative materials that can aid in the development or enhancement of your Environmental, Health, Safety and Security Program. Both pre-packaged and customized options are available. Our only intent is to help you succeed.
