COSMOS EHSS PROVIDES TRAINING

Cosmos EHSS Consulting has expanded our training offerings by providing more eLearning alternatives. Visit our eLearning Center for a list of courses. Click HERE.

In addition to our current courses, we are offering you the opportunity to design your own course curriculum. We will work with you to determine what training is necessary, what type of training best addresses the risks you face and how your work force will adapt to each training variation. We can work with you to utilize your own Learning Management System or to use ours either on your servers or ours so that you may administer, monitor, document and report out on all EHSS related training. We want to simplify your compliance requirements and maximize your efficiency.
