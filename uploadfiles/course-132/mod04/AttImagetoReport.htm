Attaching Images - Attaching Images to the Issue Report  

After modifying your image and adding the appropriate amount of detail, you'll need to attach the image to the report. Depending upon which version of CAGRIS you're using, you'll do this by either browsing for and attaching the image or attaching the image after modifying it within the application. To attach the image, click the Attach button. In many cases, the images will appear in the report in the order in which they were attached.
