Report Issue - Initial Report Information  

When reporting a new issue, there is important information that must be included prior to reporting on the Customer Concern or Repair Action details. In Lesson 3 you learned that this initial reporting information could be submitted using different layouts, depending upon which version of CAGRIS you use to report the issue. In general, the information that needs to be reported is the same. Here are some examples of the information that you will need to report using one of th CAGRIS versions.
  • Determine if the issue pertains to Powertrain or NonPowertrain
  • Determine if the issue was Pre-Delivery Quality or Reliability
  • For effected vehicles, report the:
    • VIN
    • Mileage
    • Month/Day/Hour
    • Dealer
    • Parts Replaced

  • Indicate the Labor Operations (LOP) and/or Technical Service Bulletin (TSB) number
