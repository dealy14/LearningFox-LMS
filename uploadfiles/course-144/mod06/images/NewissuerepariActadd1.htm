Repair Action: Additional Details

  

To complete the Repair Action field, document all other details you discovered during your investigation into the issue.

  • Explain the process that led to your root cause explanation
  • Verify the customer complaint
  • Quantify the issue: Use specific measurements such as, "the hole is one inch in diameter" rather than "the hole is small".
  • Determine how the part can be identified
    • If the part has a number on it, record the number and take a picture of it.
    • If the part has markings on it, document the markings and take pictures of them.
