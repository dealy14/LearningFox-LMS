Quality Narrative Analyzer - Searching for Reports on QNA  

Use QNA to your benefit when researching any field quality issue. QNA allows you to search for CAGRIS reports by keyword, report number, date, vehicle information, LOP, Part number, dealer, etc. Use QNA to find similar CAGRIS reports to identify what happened, what was done, and how the problem was fixed. You can save and group your searches and even email search results or groups to your colleagues.
