Attaching Images - Uploading Images from the Digital Camera  

As mentioned in Report Issue, you will be taking very detailed digital pictures during your investigation into the vehicle repair process.

You should always include an image with your CAGRIS issue report, even if it's just a picture of the part that was changed. You should take digital pictures at the highest possible resolution your camera will allow. After taking the pictures, you'll need to upload the pictures from your camera to your computer. There are a few different ways to upload digital pictures, depending upon which type of camera you're using.
