What do you do if you suspect an incident?

Contact the Network Operations Center (NOC) by calling 571-272-6700 or e-mailing cirt@uspto.gov.

Let the NOC or CIRT know the following:

  • Location of incident
  • Your name, title and organization
  • Date and time of report filing
  • Date and time of incident
  • Details of incident (who, what, when, where, how, and why)
Note: Don’t send e-mail from a computer that may be infected with malicious code!
