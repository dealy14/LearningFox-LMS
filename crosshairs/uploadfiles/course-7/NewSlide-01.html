Sensitive Information Responsibilities

You are responsible for the proper control, classification, handling, marking and disposition of sensitive information residing on your computer, on removable media, and on printouts that you possess:

Protect yourself and the USPTO by doing the following:

  • For all documents containing National Security markings or documents ordered to be filed under seal pursuant to a protective order, see the DOC Security Manual, Chapters 20, for guidance.

  • Do not transmit classified data, data subject to a secrecy order, or data under seal through the Internet or via e-mail unless it has been properly protected through encryption software. Contact the IT Security Policy Division if you need assistance.

  • Do not store or transmit sensitive data without proper protection as defined in applicable Federal laws and regulations.

  • Do not post sensitive data in discussion groups or bulletin boards.
