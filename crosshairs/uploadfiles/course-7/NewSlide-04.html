Personally Identifiable Information (PII) (Con't.)

Defining exactly what information falls into the category of PII, establishing where PII resides within an organization and deciding the best methods for protecting PII are all very complex tasks.

As of early 2007, USPTO and other governmental agencies were still in the process of developing more specific definitions for PII and establishing protective measures for PII. While the previous two slides provide an excellent starting point and offer good, basic security measures for protecting PII, they are by no means the last words on the subject. As the agency refines its definition of PII and determines best practices for keeping PII safe, you will be provided with more information and additional training so that you can better understand and protect PII.
