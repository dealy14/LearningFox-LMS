Introduction

Before you are given access to a USPTO computer system, and each year thereafter as a refresher, you must complete an IT Security User awareness course. This course is to ensure that you use your IT system in a proper and secure manner. This course includes the following topics:

  • Policy and Basics

  • Goals of IT Security

  • Threats, Vulnerabilities, Countermeasures

  • Sensitive Information

  • Personally Identifiable Information

  • Risk

  • Incident Response

  • Use of IT Resources

  • Your Responsibility
