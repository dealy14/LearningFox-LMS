Answer 5: E-mail Security

Whether or not you know who sent you the e-mail with the attachment, you should always scan an attachment before opening it. More virus infections occur when people open unsolicited e-mail attachments than by any other method. Suspicious e-mails with attachments should never be opened; they may contain a virus. In some cases, even viewing the e-mail in the preview pane of an e-mail application can cause a system to be infected.
