Federal Laws and Regulations

There are many Federal Laws and Regulations that are associated with computer security. These include the following:

  • Clinger-Cohen Act

  • Computer Fraud and Abuse Act

  • E-Government Act of 2002

  • Federal Information Security Management Act (FISMA)

  • OMB A-130

  • Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995

  • Privacy Act of 1974

  • Patriot Act
