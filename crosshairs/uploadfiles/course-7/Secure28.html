IT Resources - Remote Access

The USPTO is recognized for its Work at Home program. This program allows people to work away from the office and remotely access the USPTO IT systems. Each remote access point equals a vulnerability point for USPTO. It is still very important to keep the USPTO and its systems secure. If you are working at home, please do the following:

  • Keep systems used for remote access patched with most recent updates.

  • Keep virus software updated.

  • Use strong passwords.

  • Use a firewall.

  • Do not attempt to defeat or reconfigure the security settings of a USPTO supplied computer.

Follow your business area's policies on working at home. If you are unsure how to do any of the above items, contact the USPTO Help Desk.
