Cyber Security Incidents

A cyber security incident broadly refers to an adverse event resulting from malicious technical activity that results in one or more of the following:

  • Loss of data confidentiality

  • Disruption of data or system integrity

  • Disruption or denial of availability

Examples include:

  • Intrusions (e.g. unauthorized modifications or unknown files or tools, or unusual activity like after hours log ins by unauthorized personnel)

  • Denial of Service (e.g. not having access to a system, or not being able to receive e-mail because someone has locked up your account by sending you lots of large files)

  • Malicious code (e.g. viruses, worms)
