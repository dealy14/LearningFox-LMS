Threat: Malicious Code – Hints and Tips

Protect yourself and the USPTO by doing the following:

  • Report occurrences of malicious code attacks to the PTO Network Operations Center by calling 571-272-6700 or e-mailing cirt@uspto.gov.

  • Don’t disable your real-time virus scanner. Contact the Help Desk at 571-272-9000 if you receive a message saying your virus signatures are out of date.

  • Always scan a floppy disk (or other removable media) from an unknown source for viruses before using it. (To scan a file or disk, double click on the Norton Antivirus icon in the notification section of the taskbar.)

  • Delete spam, chain, and other junk e-mail without forwarding.

  • Follow your business area regulations for downloading files from unknown or suspicious sources.
