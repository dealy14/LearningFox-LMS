IT Resources - Internet Don’ts

Do not use your Internet access for the following:

  • Posting USPTO or other DOC information to external newsgroups, bulletin boards, or other public forums without authority from USPTO Management.

  • Accessing sites with continuous data streams (e.g. audio or video such as Pointcast).

  • Accessing sites known for hacker attacks or hacker activity.

  • Accessing or participating in Internet Relay Chat sessions.

  • Obtaining software obtained in violation of the appropriate vendor's patent, copyrights, trade secret, or license agreement.

  • Allowing any unauthorized person to access a USPTO or DOC-owned system for the purpose of Internet access.

  • Downloading shareware/freeware software or executable programs unless authorized by CIO management as part of your normal job function.
