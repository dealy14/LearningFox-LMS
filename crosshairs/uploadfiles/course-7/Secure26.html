IT Resources - Internet Hints and Tips

Protect yourself and the USPTO by doing the following:

  • Take measures to ensure sensitive data or materials are protected from unauthorized disclosure and are not transmitted across the Internet without an appropriate level of security.

  • Be aware that the uspto.gov address is recorded by every www site visited and may thus do the following:
      • Create the presumption that a USPTO employee is using government resources for non-government activities on government time.
      • Create the incorrect presumption that the user speaks with authority for the USPTO regardless of the presence of any disclaimer.
  • Be aware that Internet transmissions are public, and may be monitored, intercepted, and modified.
