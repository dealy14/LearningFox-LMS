Sensitive Information (Cont.)

Sensitive information may be designated or marked "For Official Use Only" (FOUO), or "Sensitive but Unclassified" (SBU).  USPTO information and system owners should determine what protection is appropriate for the sensitive information in their business operations.

Patent applications and related information are specifically protected under 35 USC 122 & 18 USC 2071.

    Applications for patents shall be kept in confidence by the Patent and Trademark Office and no information concerning the same given without authority of the applicant or owner unless necessary to carry out the provisions of any Act of Congress or in such special circumstances as may be determined by the Commissioner. 35 USC 122

Sensitive information may also be classified as national security information and be subject to very stringent security protection. For information on how classified information differs from sensitive information, go to http://www.osec.doc.gov/cio/ITSIT/DOC-IT-Security-Program-Policy.htm#P1981_223062 .
